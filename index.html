Two types of alerts

The Confluence tab sends alerts when a full MTF scan passes your threshold. The Watchdog tab fires only on state transitions — the first moment a ticker crosses into a strong setup.

🎯
Confluence Alert — runs every 5/15/30m, scans all tickers against 8 MTF conditions, sends Grade A/B setups with trade levels
🔔
Watchdog Alert — watches specific tickers continuously, fires only when status changes from non-qualifying → qualifying (no duplicate spam)
⏱
1-hour cooldown — once alerted, same ticker won't fire again for 60 minutes even if auto-scan re-runs
📐
Trade levels included — every alert includes Entry, Stop Loss (1.5×ATR), T1 (2R), and T2 (4R) automatically
